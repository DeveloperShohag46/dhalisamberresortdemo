Our Story

The Art of Hospitality

Nestled in the serene landscapes, Dhali's Amber Nivaas is a sanctuary of peace and luxury. Our journey began with a vision to create a space where guests could escape the hustle of everyday life and reconnect with nature while enjoying world-class amenities.

Founded in 2005, our resort has grown from a small retreat to a premier destination for travelers seeking authentic experiences. We take pride in our commitment to sustainable luxury, ensuring that our operations respect and preserve the natural beauty that surrounds us.

At Dhali's Amber Nivaas, we believe that true luxury lies in the details - from the locally sourced ingredients in our cuisine to the handcrafted furnishings in our rooms. Every aspect of your stay is thoughtfully designed to create memories that last a lifetime.

Dhali's Commitment

Sustainability

We are committed to sustainable practices that minimize our environmental footprint while maximizing guest comfort. From solar power to water conservation, we strive to protect our natural surroundings.

Community

We believe in giving back to the local community. Our resort employs local staff and sources ingredients from nearby farms, supporting the regional economy and providing authentic experiences.

Excellence

We are dedicated to providing exceptional service that exceeds expectations. Our staff undergoes rigorous training to ensure that every guest interaction reflects our commitment to excellence.
